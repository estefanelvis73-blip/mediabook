Common Questions

FAQ

    ```
  • How do I book a session with you? +
    Simply choose your service, click "Book This", fill in the form and send via email or add directly to Google Calendar. I'll reply within 24 hours to confirm availability and discuss the details of your session personally.
  • How long until I receive my edited photos? +
    Portrait and street sessions are typically delivered within 5–7 business days. Fashion and editorial work takes 7–10 days. Wedding galleries are fully edited and delivered within 3–4 weeks after the event. Video reels are delivered within 10–14 business days including colour grading.
  • Do you shoot outside Frankfurt? +
    Absolutely. I'm based in Frankfurt am Main but regularly work throughout the Rhine-Main region — Wiesbaden, Darmstadt, Mainz and beyond. For destinations further afield, travel costs are discussed transparently before booking. International projects are considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • What is your cancellation policy? +
    Cancellations made more than 7 days before the session are fully refunded. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 30% late-cancellation fee. Rescheduling is always welcome — please give at least 48 hours' notice and I'll find a new date that works for both of us.
  • What should I bring or wear to my portrait session? +
    I always send a personalised prep guide after booking, but the short version: bring 2–3 outfit options in neutral or muted tones, avoid large logos, and come as you are — relaxed and ready to enjoy the process. The best photos happen when you forget the camera is there, and it's my job to make that happen.
```
